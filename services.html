Ice Cream Flavours

An ice cream shop is a business that primarily sells ice cream but may also specialize in other frozen treats
such as milkshakes, gelato, frozen yogurt, and sorbet. Each frozen treat has a unique preparation method.
Ice cream is a calcium-rich food, and calcium helps keep your body strong and boosts your metabolism. Adding a higher fat ice cream
will help make you feel fuller longer too. Just be mindful of your portion size!
